Our Car: The FW46

The Williams FW46 is our latest Formula 1 challenger for the 2024 season. Designed with cutting-edge aerodynamics and powered by a state-of-the-art Mercedes power unit, the FW46 represents the pinnacle of our engineering capabilities. Key features include an innovative front wing design, optimized sidepods for improved cooling, and a refined rear diffuser for enhanced downforce. With a lightweight carbon fiber chassis and advanced suspension system, the FW46 is built for both speed and agility on the track.
